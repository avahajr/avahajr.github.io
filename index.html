About me

Hi there! I'm Ava Hajratwala and I'm a computer science major at Barnard College of Columbia University. I'm based in NYC, but I'm orignially from just outside of Ann Arbor, Michigan.

I study computer science because I love how self-directed it is. Each new idea (or fundamentals course), regardless of experience level, is a launching point for seemingly endless new skills and projects. When I was a kid, for example, a casual interest in video games led me down a rabbithole to rudamentary game design in Scratch. And now in college, an introductory lesson in computer networking formed the foundation for me teaching myself frontend web development!
